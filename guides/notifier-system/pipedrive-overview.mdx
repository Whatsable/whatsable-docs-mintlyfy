# Notifyer System Integration with Pipedrive

<Frame caption="Automate your WhatsApp messaging workflows with Pipedrive and Notifyer System">
  <img src="/images/pipedrive.svg" className="rounded-xl" />
</Frame>

<Tip>
  This guide walks you through connecting Notifyer System with Pipedrive to create powerful automated WhatsApp messaging workflows for your sales and CRM processes
</Tip>

## Prerequisites

Before getting started, make sure you have:

<CardGroup cols={2}>

  <Card title="Pipedrive Account" icon="user">

</Tip>

## Setting up your Notifyer System account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete the Embedding Process">
    Before sending WhatsApp messages, you must complete the platform [embedding process](/guides/notifier-system/embedding-process), which connects your WhatsApp Business account to Notifyer System.

    <Info>
      The embedding process is required by Meta to ensure proper business verification and compliance with WhatsApp Business Platform policies.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set up message templates">
    Notifyer System provides two methods for sending WhatsApp messages:

    <Accordion title="Option A: Template messages" defaultOpen={true}>
      <p>WhatsApp templates are pre-approved message formats that allow for personalization while maintaining compliance with WhatsApp policies.</p>
      
      <Steps>
        <Step title="Navigate to Templates">
          Go to **Your Templates** in your Notifyer dashboard
        </Step>
        <Step title="Create Template">
          Click the **Create Template** tab at the top of the page
        </Step>
        <Step title="Fill out the form">
          Complete the template creation form with the following details:
          
          <ResponseField name="Template Name" type="string" required>
            Choose a descriptive name for internal reference
          </ResponseField>
          <ResponseField name="Language" type="string" required>
            Choose your template's primary language
          </ResponseField>
          <ResponseField name="Category" type="string" required>
            Select the appropriate message category
          </ResponseField>          
          <ResponseField name="Header" type="image | document | video">
            Optional: Add an image, document, or video header
          </ResponseField>
          <ResponseField name="Message Body" type="string" required>
            Craft your message content
          </ResponseField>
          <ResponseField name="Variables" type="string">
            Add placeholders using `{{1}}`, `{{2}}` format for personalization
          </ResponseField>          
          <ResponseField name="Buttons" type="button">
            Optional: Configure call-to-action buttons
          </ResponseField>
        </Step>
        <Step title="Submit for review">
          Click **Preview and Submit**
        </Step>
      </Steps>

      <Frame caption="Template Creation Interface">
        <img src="/images/notifyer_template_create.png" alt="Template Creation Interface" />
      </Frame>

      <Warning>
        Templates typically get reviewed within 24 hours. Creating compliant templates that avoid promotional language increases approval chances.
      </Warning>
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Option B: Non-template messages">
      <p>For simpler communications, you can send non-template messages that include:</p>
      
      <CardGroup cols={2}>
        <Card title="Text" icon="message-text">
          Plain text messages within the 24-hour window
        </Card>
        <Card title="Images" icon="image">
          Photos and graphics in supported formats
        </Card>
        <Card title="Documents" icon="file-pdf">
          PDFs, Word docs, and other file types
        </Card>
        <Card title="Videos" icon="video">
          MP4 and other supported video formats
        </Card>
      </CardGroup>

      <Note>
        Non-template messages can only be sent within the 24-hour customer service window after a customer initiates contact with your business.
      </Note>
    </Accordion>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect Notifyer System with Pipedrive">
    Follow these simple steps to integrate your Pipedrive account with Notifyer System:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Access the Integration Page">
        In your Notifyer dashboard, go to [**Connect to Pipedrive**](https://console.notifyer-systems.com/pipedrive).
      </Step>

      <Step title="Initiate the Connection">
        Click the 'Connect Pipedrive Organization' button.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Authorize Access">
        You'll be redirected to Pipedrive's authorization page. Review the permissions and click 'Authorize' to grant Notifyer System access to your Pipedrive organization.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Confirm Connection">
        After authorization, you'll be automatically redirected back to your Notifyer System dashboard. Look for the connection status showing 'Connected to Pipedrive' to confirm the integration was successful.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Frame caption="Connecting Notifyer System with Pipedrive">
      <img src="/images/pipedrive-connection.gif" alt="Connecting Notifyer System with Pipedrive" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Integrating with Pipedrive

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your integration method">
    You can integrate Notifyer System with Pipedrive using one of these methods:

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Via Automation Platforms" icon="diagram-project">
        Use Make, Zapier, or n8n to connect Pipedrive with Notifyer System
      </Card>
      <Card title="Via Webhooks" icon="webhook">
        Set up Pipedrive webhooks to trigger Notifyer System API calls
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    <Tip>
      For most users, using an automation platform like Make or Zapier provides the easiest setup experience.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up Pipedrive triggers">
    Configure Pipedrive to trigger WhatsApp messages based on your sales activities:

    <Accordion title="Common trigger examples" defaultOpen>
      <CardGroup cols={2}>
        <Card title="New Deal" icon="handshake">
          Send welcome messages when new deals are created
        </Card>
        <Card title="Deal Stage Changed" icon="arrows-rotate">
          Notify contacts when deal moves to a new stage
        </Card>
        <Card title="Deal Won" icon="trophy">
          Send congratulations and next steps when deals close
        </Card>
        <Card title="Activity Created" icon="calendar-plus">
          Send reminders when new activities are scheduled
        </Card>
      </CardGroup>
    </Accordion>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Map Pipedrive data to Notifyer System">
    Connect your Pipedrive fields to Notifyer System message parameters:

    <ResponseField name="Phone Number" type="string" required>
      Map from Pipedrive person or organization phone field (ensure format includes country code)
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="Template Variables" type="string" optional>
      Map Pipedrive fields (name, deal value, etc.) to template variables
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="Deal Information" type="object" optional>
      Use deal title, stage, value, or custom fields for personalization
    </ResponseField>

    <Info>
      Make sure phone numbers in Pipedrive include country codes (e.g., +1234567890) for successful message delivery.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test your integration">
    1. Create a test deal or activity in Pipedrive
    2. Verify the WhatsApp message is sent correctly
    3. Check message content and variable substitution
    4. Confirm delivery in your Notifyer System dashboard

    <Check>
      Your Pipedrive-Notifyer System integration is now active! Sales activities will automatically trigger WhatsApp messages.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Integration via automation platforms

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Using Make">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create scenario">
        1. Log in to Make
        2. Create a new scenario
        3. Add Pipedrive as your trigger module
        4. Select your trigger event (e.g., "Watch Deals")
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Notifyer System module">
        1. Click **+** to add a module
        2. Search for "Notifyer System"
        3. Select your action (Template or Non-template message)
        4. Connect with your Notifyer System API key
      </Step>
      <Step title="Map fields">
        1. Map Pipedrive phone field to recipient
        2. Map relevant Pipedrive data to template variables
        3. Test the mapping with sample data
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Info>
      See our [Make integration guide](/guides/notifier-system/make-overview) for detailed instructions.
    </Info>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Using Zapier">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create Zap">
        1. Log in to Zapier
        2. Create a new Zap
        3. Choose Pipedrive as your trigger app
        4. Select trigger event (e.g., "New Deal")
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Notifyer System action">
        1. Add a new action step
        2. Search for Notifyer System or WhatsAble
        3. Connect your Notifyer System account
        4. Choose your message action
      </Step>
      <Step title="Configure message">
        1. Map phone number from Pipedrive
        2. Select your template
        3. Fill in template variables from Pipedrive fields
        4. Test the Zap
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Info>
      See our [Zapier integration guide](/guides/notifier-system/zapier-overview) for detailed instructions.
    </Info>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Using n8n">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create workflow">
        1. Log in to n8n
        2. Create a new workflow
        3. Add Pipedrive trigger node
        4. Configure trigger event
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Notifyer node">
        1. Add WhatsAble node
        2. Choose Notifyer System action
        3. Configure authentication with API key
        4. Select message type
      </Step>
      <Step title="Connect data">
        1. Map Pipedrive data to message fields
        2. Configure template variables
        3. Test the workflow
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Info>
      See our [n8n integration guide](/guides/notifier-system/n8n-overview) for detailed instructions.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use proper phone formatting" icon="phone">
    Ensure all phone numbers in Pipedrive include country codes and follow international format (+1234567890).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Personalize messages" icon="user">
    Use Pipedrive custom fields to personalize WhatsApp messages with deal-specific information.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Follow up strategically" icon="bullseye">
    Set up automated follow-ups based on deal stages to maintain engagement without overwhelming contacts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Test before activating" icon="vial">
    Always test your integration with sample data before activating it for live deals.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Monitor delivery" icon="chart-line">
    Regularly check message delivery rates in your Notifyer dashboard to ensure optimal performance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Messages not sending?" icon="circle-exclamation">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Verify API key">
        Ensure your Notifyer System API key is entered correctly
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check phone format">
        Confirm phone numbers include country codes (e.g., +14155552671)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify trigger">
        Check that your Pipedrive trigger is firing correctly
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check subscription">
        Verify your Notifyer subscription is active with available credits
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Variables not populating?" icon="text">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check field mapping">
        Verify Pipedrive fields are correctly mapped to template variables
      </Step>
      <Step title="Test data">
        Ensure your test deal has data in the fields you're mapping
      </Step>
      <Step title="Field names">
        Double-check that custom field names match exactly in your mapping
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Duplicate messages?" icon="copy">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check trigger settings">
        Review your automation platform trigger to avoid duplicate events
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add filters">
        Use filters to prevent messages from sending multiple times for the same event
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review workflow">
        Ensure you don't have multiple active workflows for the same trigger
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
