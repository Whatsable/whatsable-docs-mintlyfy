## Integrating the WhatsAble Suite with n8n

All three platforms in the WhatsAble Suite integrate with n8n through a similar workflow pattern:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up platform account">
    Create and configure your account on your chosen WhatsApp platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify phone number">
    Complete the required verification steps for your WhatsApp number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Obtain API key">
    Generate an API key from your platform's dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure n8n">
    Add the platform's node in n8n and enter your API credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build workflow">
    Design your automation workflow with triggers and actions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and activate">
    Test your workflow configuration and activate it for production use.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a business account for the WhatsAble Suite?">
    <p>It depends on which platform in the WhatsAble Suite you choose:</p>
    <ul className="list-disc pl-6">
      <li><strong>WhatsAble:</strong> Works with regular numbers - no business account required</li>
      <li><strong>Notifier:</strong> Requires a business number for full functionality</li>
      <li><strong>Notifyer System:</strong> Requires complete business API integration</li>
    </ul>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How does n8n connect to the WhatsAble Suite?">
    <p>The WhatsAble Suite integrates with n8n through dedicated nodes:</p>
    <ul className="list-disc pl-6">
      <li>Each platform has its own dedicated node in the n8n library</li>
      <li>API credentials from your chosen platform are required for connection</li>
      <li>Once connected, you can trigger messaging actions from any n8n workflow</li>
      <li>All platforms support dynamic variables from previous nodes in your workflow</li>
    </ul>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What media types can I send through the n8n integration?">
    <p>All platforms in the WhatsAble Suite support standard media types when integrated with n8n:</p>
    <ul className="list-disc pl-6">
      <li><strong>Images:</strong> JPG, PNG (up to 5MB)</li>
      <li><strong>Videos:</strong> MP4, 3GP (up to 16MB)</li>
      <li><strong>Documents:</strong> PDF, DOC, XLSX, etc. (up to 100MB)</li>
      <li><strong>Audio:</strong> MP3, OGG, etc.</li>
    </ul>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I use dynamic content from my n8n workflows?">
    <p>Yes, all platforms in the WhatsAble Suite support dynamic content from n8n workflows:</p>
    <ul className="list-disc pl-6">
      <li><strong>WhatsAble:</strong> Use variables directly in message content with no restrictions</li>
      <li><strong>Notifier:</strong> Supports variables in message body and media captions</li>
      <li><strong>Notifyer System:</strong> Offers template variables for approved templates and regular variables for non-template messages</li>
    </ul>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
